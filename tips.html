(hopefully)helpful tips for your hand lettering!

tip no. 1:

be loose! if you're nervous of messing something up, don't worry about it! if you're really scared of messing something up, go ahead and use pencil first so you can always fix any mistakes, not that mistakes are bad AT ALL!

tip no. 2

practice practice practice! practice makes perfect, always! whenever i was bored and was doodling, all i did was practice the lettering, which helped so much in improving!

tip no. 3

whenever you feel like your finished project is messed up or bad, THATS OK!! theres always more pieces of paper!
